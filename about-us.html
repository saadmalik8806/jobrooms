The
Everything OnDemand
Search Engine

JOBROOMS is a human-powered search engine that instantly connects you with everything you need through a phone call with an expert or seller. Imagine asking a question about a car trouble and in seconds, having a local mechanic on your screen diagnosing the problem live! Or imagine craving a delicious pizza and boom – you’re face-to-face with a friendly pizza chef showcasing their mouthwatering creations. That’s the future JOBROOMS is bringing to life.

Forget juggling multiple apps, searching the web endlessly, booking appointments, and waiting on hold! JOBROOMS is your one-stop, human-powered connection to everything you need, instantly. Need a job, food, groceries, or services like dental care, accounting, cleaning, plumbing, transportation, or legal help? Simply submit your query, request, or shopping list, and you’ll be on a live call with an expert or seller in a few seconds for real-time assistance.

JOBROOMS is “The everything-a-call-away platform” representing a vision of what search technology could become in the future – combining human involvement, advancements in artificial intelligence, big data, and real-time processing. In essence, JOBROOMS aims to be a hybrid search engine, combining the best of human intuition and AI capabilities to deliver a search experience that goes way beyond what we’re currently used to.

Our mission is to make information, every service, and product instantly accessible to everyone. We meticulously organise people, businesses, and organisations on a single platform. This foundation fosters seamless connections, creating a unified and efficient economic ecosystem.
